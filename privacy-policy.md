# Privacy Policy

Smart Alarm · Effective date: May 4, 2026

Smart Alarm is a local-first alarm app that helps users wake up by completing challenges such as photo proof, walking, shaking, or memory tasks. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

## Data We Access

- **Camera:** Used for photo-proof alarms, including reference and proof captures.
- **Location:** Used only while the app is open for walking challenges as a movement fallback.
- **Activity recognition / step data:** Used to count steps during walking challenges.
- **Notifications:** Used to alert you when alarms are due.
- **Alarm and challenge settings:** Stored locally so your alarms and preferences work on your device.
- **Photos captured for photo proof:** Stored locally on your device for challenge comparison.

## How We Use Data

Smart Alarm uses this data only to provide alarm and wake-up challenge features. Photos, location, step data, alarm settings, and challenge history are processed locally on your device. They are not uploaded to a server by Smart Alarm.

## Android Permissions

- **CAMERA:** Photo-proof challenge capture.
- **ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION:** Foreground walking challenge movement fallback.
- **ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION:** Step counting for walking challenges.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS:** Alarm notifications.
- **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM:** Reliable scheduled alarms.
- **USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT:** Show the alarm screen over the lock screen where Android allows it.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK:** Alarm ringing audio while active.
- **WAKE_LOCK:** Keep the alarm challenge visible while ringing.
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED:** Restore enabled alarms after device reboot.
- **VIBRATE:** Alarm vibration.

Smart Alarm does not use microphone recording. Android microphone permission is blocked in the app configuration.

## Sharing

Smart Alarm does not sell personal data and does not share your photos, location, step data, alarm settings, or challenge data with third parties.

## Storage and Retention

Alarm settings, challenge settings, and photo-proof references are stored locally on your device. You can delete alarms or uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.

## Children

Smart Alarm is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
